▸ CANDIDATE SIGNATURES
How it works: one drone's LoRa ping reflects off the terrain below; a second drone listens for the echo and runs it through a Doppler FFT. Chest-wall motion from breathing and heartbeat shows up as a faint periodic shift in that echo — the system fuses repeated, noisy detections per grid cell into a single rising probability of detection.
